Short Bio

I am currently working as an Algorithm Technology Developer at Academy of Satellite Application Innovation, specializing in reinforcement learning applications for satellite systems.

Previously, I worked as a Senior Algorithm Researcher at Great Wall Motors (2023-2025), focusing on autonomous driving and decision-making systems.

I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Software Technology at Dalian University of Technology in 2023. I also had the opportunity to work as a visiting Ph.D. student at Singapore Management University under the supervision of Prof. Pradeep Varakantham (2021-2022).

Research Interests: My research focuses on reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems, causal inference, and their real-world applications. I am particularly interested in:

  • Offline reinforcement learning and off-policy evaluation
  • Multi-agent coordination and communication
  • Causal inference in learning systems
  • Adversarial robustness in RL
  • Applications in autonomous systems and optimization
