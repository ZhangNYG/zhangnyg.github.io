Short Bio

 I am currently working on autonomous driving filed as a senior algorithm researcher at (Great Wall Motor Company Limited - GWM) Haomo AI Technology Co., Ltd., where I am responsible for decision planning and guide line related work. I get the Ph.D. degree with the School of Software Technology, Dalian University of Technology, China. My research interest includes reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems, causal inference, and generative models (VAE, Diffusion model).
